## Why Elderly People Are Targeted By Scammers

**Hard truth:** Your parents are specifically targeted because:

### Psychological Vulnerability:
- Trust authority figures (HMRC, police, banks)
- Politeness (don't hang up - it's "rude")
- Slower to spot deception (cognitive aging)
- Fear of being a burden (don't tell family)

### Practical Vulnerability:
- Home alone during daytime hours
- Answer landlines (don't screen calls)
- Have savings (target-rich)
- Less tech-savvy (can't verify online)

### Emotional Vulnerability:
- Fear of losing independence
- Shame about being "fooled"
- Isolation (fewer people to check with)
- Anxiety about finances

**Your parents aren't weak. Scammers are professional criminals who exploit normal human psychology.**

## What Doesn't Work (And Why)

### ❌ Smartphones with "Senior Mode"
**Why they fail:**
- Still get scam calls
- Learning curve (they're used to landlines)
- Small screens (hard to read caller ID)
- Apps to update (complexity)
- Expensive

### ❌ Traditional Call Blockers
**Why they fail:**
- Only block known numbers
- Scammers use thousands of new numbers daily
- Legitimate calls get blocked too
- Requires constant updates

### ❌ "Just Don't Answer Unknown Numbers"
**Why it fails:**
- Doctor calls from different numbers
- Pharmacy uses mobiles
- Grandkids calling from friend's phone
- Hospital doesn't always show up
- They can't risk missing important calls

### ❌ BT Call Protect / TrueCall Boxes
**Why they fail:**
- Blocks ALL unknowns (including important calls)
- Complicated setup
- False sense of security
- Still requires user decisions
