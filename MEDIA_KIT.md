## The Product in One Sentence

ScamBlocker is an AI-powered digital landline service that screens unknown callers *before* your phone rings, preventing scammers from ever reaching vulnerable people—SONIQ Labs' first B2C product built on enterprise CPaaS infrastructure.

## The Problem We Solve

- UK families lose £1.2 billion annually to telephone fraud
- 81% of successful scams begin with a phone call
- Elderly and vulnerable adults are primary targets
- Traditional call-blocking is reactive—damage happens first
- BT PSTN shutdown in 2027 forces migration to digital

## Our Solution

**Pre-emptive AI screening** that intercepts calls before connection:
1. Unknown number calls
2. AI asks: "Who are you? Why are you calling?"
3. Real-time analysis detects scam patterns
4. Decision: Connect, block, or alert family
5. Your phone never rings for scam calls

## Key Features

✓ **Pre-Call Screening** - AI interviews callers before connecting  
✓ **Payment Blocker™** - Prevents phone-based payments  
✓ **Call Memory** - Recognizes legitimate repeat callers  
✓ **Whitelist Protection** - Family/friends connect immediately  
✓ **Hard Guardrails** - Blocks known scam patterns  
✓ **Appointed Contacts** - Family oversight without control  
✓ **Complete Landline Replacement** - No copper wire needed
